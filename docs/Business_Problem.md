# Business Problem Statement

## Background

Agriculture plays a vital role in food security and the economy. Farmers often make decisions about crop selection and resource allocation based on experience or historical trends. However, changing weather patterns, soil conditions, and environmental factors make it difficult to accurately estimate crop yield before harvest.

## Problem Statement

Farmers and agricultural organizations lack a reliable, data-driven system to estimate crop yield before the harvesting season. This uncertainty can lead to poor planning, financial losses, inefficient resource utilization, and disruptions in the food supply chain.

## Proposed Solution

Develop a machine learning-based Crop Yield Prediction and Agricultural Decision Support System that predicts crop yield using historical crop production, weather, and soil data. The system will assist users in making informed agricultural decisions through predictive analytics and an interactive dashboard.

## Expected Business Value

- Improve crop yield estimation accuracy.
- Support better farming decisions.
- Reduce financial risks.
- Assist government agencies in agricultural planning.
- Improve food supply chain forecasting.
