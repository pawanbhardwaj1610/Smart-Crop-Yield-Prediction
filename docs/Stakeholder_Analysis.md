# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders

### Farmers
- Estimate crop yield before harvest.
- Select suitable crops.
- Plan fertilizer and irrigation usage.

### Government Agencies
- Forecast agricultural production.
- Plan food storage and distribution.
- Develop agricultural policies.

### Agricultural Companies
- Forecast demand for seeds and fertilizers.
- Improve inventory planning.

### Food Supply Chain Organizations
- Estimate production volumes.
- Improve logistics and storage planning.

### Insurance Companies
- Assess agricultural risks.
- Estimate compensation for crop losses.

## Secondary Stakeholders

- Researchers
- Agricultural Consultants
- Financial Institutions
